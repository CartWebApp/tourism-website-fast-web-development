"Experience Everything this World has to Offer"

    We have lived by our late founder, Harold Nicholas “Würstchen” Hähnchenflügel’s final words for the past 60 odd years, serving the people and delivering them once-in-a-lifetime experiences for the sake of spreading joy to the world. Beginning in a quaint 18th century American countryside slaughterhouse our organization has had grand goals from such humble beginnings. We offer the unique opportunity to experience the globe, from the holy walled city of Rome to deep in the mysterious Orient.

Cross into "The Borderland"

     Ukraine is filled with opportunities to explore a rich culture and history. The larger cities are home to many famous cafes and restaurants, and those interested in an adventure can take guided tours of the city of Pripyat, the site of the infamous Chernobyl Nuclear Disaster. Fans of architecture will love touring Kiev, the capital of Ukraine.
