The Modern Symbol of Ukraine

     Originating from a 12th century Ruthenian flag the modern day Ukrainian flag features a mix of "Strong Azure" (#0057b7) and Yellow (#ffd700). These colors get their historical significance from pre-christian paganism, in which blue and yellow represented water and fire respectively. The flag itself time and time again has fought many battles against unparalled opposition in the form of redesign over redesign coinciding with the flowing change of its nationsfor it's persistence under adversity and pressure it has staked i claim countless times over as the predominant representation of its home country.
