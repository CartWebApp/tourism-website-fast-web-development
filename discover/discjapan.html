Shinto Shrine

According to the Japan Guide, Shinto Shrines are places of worship and bring good fortune; not money, but to have a better life and have good luck. Shinto Shrines is a religion that praises the Shinto Gods called ( Kami). Tourists can visit the Shinto Shrine during special events for example: New Year, Setsubun, Shichigosan, or other festivals. Did you know that Shinto Shrine can also have wedding ceremonies or new born babies can get blessed in the shrines.

Shinto Shrine part 2

According to the BBC, Shinto Shrines are spiritual homes that will calm you down and relax your mind. In the text it states that, Shinto Shrines are connected to the natural world, and can include sacred groves of trees, and streams. Shinto Shrine is used to celebrate many festivals.
