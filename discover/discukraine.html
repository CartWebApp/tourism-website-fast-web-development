Hungry? Manslenitsa Week

    Ukraine is filled with opportunities to explore a rich culture and history. The larger cities are home to many famous cafes and restaurants, and those interested in an adventure can take guided tours of the city of Pripyat, the site of the infamous Chernobyl Nuclear Disaster. Fans of architecture will love touring Kiev, the capital of Ukraine.

Pripyat - Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant

    Many visitors will already recognize the photo, and even more will recognize the name. Home to one of the only 2 nuclear events to ever reach maximum severity on the International Nuclear Event Scale, Pripyat is a grim reminder of the consequences of negligence. With the radiation having dropped well below safe levels tourists are now allowed- with the guidance of trained professionals, to explore Pripyat and even stand where Reactor #4, the site of the meltdown, once stood.
